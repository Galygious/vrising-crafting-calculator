Hats[]

Hats (or head armour) are purely cosmetic and provide no stat bonuses. Most can be found rarely in Golden Chests or through Fishing. Unlike other Equipment, Hats aren't blood-bound and will be dropped on death.
Some are exclusively sold for Copper Coins, Silver Coins or Goldsun Coins at a Goods Vendor.

Bags increase the player's inventory slots. Added in the Gloomrot expansion, later reworked in Update 1.0.
All bags after Silver Thread Bag give some Silver Coin Carry Immunity.
Most are crafted at the Leatherworking Station (with the exception of Bandit Bag).
